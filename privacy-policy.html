Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 28.05.2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the RSL Mobile Helper App collects, uses, and stores information related to your use of the app. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

2. Data Collection

  • Account Information: Email address and in-game name during registration.
  • Game Data: Clan Boss rewards, shard usage, damage stats, and related game tracking data.
  • Uploaded Images: Screenshots related to Raid: Shadow Legends gameplay.
  • Advertising Data: Limited information required for showing ads through Google AdMob (e.g. device identifiers, IP address, approximate location).

3. Use of Data

Your data is used to provide and enhance app functionality, including personalized stats, AI-based suggestions, clan collaboration, and to display ads (if enabled).

4. Consent & User Choices

In regions where required (European Economic Area, United Kingdom, Switzerland, certain US states), we use Google’s certified User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request your consent before processing personal data for ads or analytics.

  • You may accept or decline personalized advertising.
  • If you decline, only non-personalized ads or no ads will be shown.
  • You can manage or withdraw your consent at any time in the app’s settings under “Manage Consent”.

5. Data Storage & Security

All data is securely stored using Supabase services with role-based access control (RLS). We implement appropriate measures to prevent unauthorized access.

6. AI Services

The AI helper uses Claude AI and only responds to Raid-related questions. No sensitive personal data is processed by the AI.

7. Third-Party Access

We use third-party services only as necessary to run the app, including:

  • Supabase (database and storage)
  • Claude AI (game-related suggestions)
  • Google AdMob (advertising, subject to user consent)

8. Account Deletion

You may delete your account and associated data at any time via the app’s settings menu.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at our Discord.
